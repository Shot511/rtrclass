What you will learn in this course:

  • Learn OpenGL version 4.x and then you will be able to create 3D applications for Windows/Linux/MacOS and mobile devices,
  • Thanks to this knowledge you will be able to use WebGL technology which is based on OpenGL,
  • You will get to know OpenGL Shading Language (GLSL) which will give you the ability to create outstanding effects.

What you will NOT learn in this course:

  • You will not learn 3D mathematics. I assume that people who are interested in programming in OpenGL are familiar with vectors, matrices, quaternions, etc.,
  • I will not be teaching programming in C/C++/Java/C#. I assume that you can programm in one of the mentioned languages.
